Bill Eager

Web Technologist based in NYC




I am a web developer based in New York City, currently working at Tumblr. In my free time I blog, make music, and enjoy cooking, cycling, and yoga.

Professional



Since 2012, I've been working on creating and developing new product features at Tumblr as a Product Engineer, as well as making Tumblr a safer, more secure service for our 150+ million blogs.

Prior to that, I spent six years at Synacor in Buffalo, NY, where I worked in a variety of engineering and leadership roles, working to deliver web portals and online TV content to millions of high-speed internet subscribers.

I have a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from the State University of New York College at Buffalo, where I graduated magna cum laude with a 3.9 GPA in major coursework.



Personal



Before the onset of my professional obligations, I've been focused on writing, recording, and performing original music. Most notable was my time as vocalist and keyboardist for Canary Girls. I also keep a short set of my solo efforts on SoundCloud.

I enjoy working on code projects in my spare time, including a keen interest in game development. Most notably, I created a clone of Minecraft in Java. Recently, I wrote a web application in Node that takes real-time telemetry data and converts it into music.

I am an aspiring writer. The extent of my writing prowess is manifested in a blog I run called Yacht Cats.

I enjoy cycling, ice hockey, and yoga. At 6'5", other people tend to not like my cycling, ice hockey, and yoga as much. I enjoy cooking, and eat a pescetarian diet.

I live in Brooklyn, NY with the love of my life, Erica. We hope to one day have a dog.
